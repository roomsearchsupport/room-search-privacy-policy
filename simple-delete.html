Delete Your Room Search Account

In accordance with Google Play Data Safety requirements, you can request the permanent closure of your account and erasure of all associated data using the options below.

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Fastest)

If you still have the app installed, follow these instructions:

  1. Open the Room Search app.
  2. Tap the 3 vertical dots on the main screen.
  3. Select Account Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account Permanently.
